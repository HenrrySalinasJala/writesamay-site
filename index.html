Writesamay Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Writesamay is a songwriting app that helps you write and refine lyrics. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains how your data is handled.

Data Storage

All your lyrics, metadata, and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not have access to your content, and nothing is stored on our servers.

AI-Powered Suggestions

When you request lyric suggestions, the selected text and your full lyrics are sent to our server, which forwards them to a third-party AI service (Anthropic Claude) to generate suggestions. Specifically, the following data is transmitted:

  • The text you selected for refinement
  • Your full lyric content (for context)
  • Optional metadata you provided (mood, genre, vocal style, AI hints)

This data is used solely to generate suggestions and is not stored on our server. Our server acts as a pass-through proxy. Anthropic's use of data sent via their API is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Per Anthropic's API terms, data sent via the API is not used to train their models.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any data

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service involved is Anthropic's Claude API, used exclusively for generating lyric suggestions when you explicitly request them. No data is sent to any third party without your action.

Children's Privacy

Writesamay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
